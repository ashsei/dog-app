Introduction


Welcome to Guess to Pet! On our platform you can enter your zip code and what type of pet you're looking for and our system will find you the cutest pets closest to you! All you have to do is guess their breed and you win points! These points are currently only for bragging rights with your friends but we will soon be adding the ability to trade your points in for discounts at local shelters! Have fun, and remember to checkout Petfinder to find more available pets in your area!


Known Bugs


  1. Score counter does not accurately reflect points after first round.
  2. Randomized answer choices do not alway fill out full dropdown, due to Math.random not always picking 1 through 5 when assigning answer choices.


Upcoming Features and Additions


  1. All bugs listed above will be fixed.
  2. Ability to save favorite pets as you play the game.
  3. Better mobile friendly styling, including mobile specific navigation.
  4. More efficient CSS.
